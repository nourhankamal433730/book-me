About The Author

VK (short for its original name VKontakte; Russian: ВКонтакте, meaning InContact) is a Russian online social media and social networking service based in Saint Petersburg. VK is available in multiple languages but it is predominantly used by Russian speakers. VK users can message each other publicly or privately, edit these messages, create groups, public pages, and events; share and tag images, audio, and video; and play browser-based games.

As of August 2018, VK had at least 500 million accounts. As of November 2022, it was the sixth most popular website in Russia. The network was also popular in Ukraine until it was banned by the Verkhovna Rada in 2017.

According to SimilarWeb, VK is the 16th most visited website in the world.

About The Book

What happens if the capacities of four scientists get downloaded into one computer? The joined brain will be able to solve the most complicated tasks at a rapid race. What if the creative potential of a few hundred more people was added to that? The artificial intellect will not just memorize it all, but produce any kind of solution, putting all together like a jigsaw puzzle. The creator of the “Einsteiner”, a bio-generated hardware working on human creative energy, has disappeared while the creativity has become the most valuable resource on earth. People willingly sell their potential for good money. As for a young talented engineer, Isaac Leroy, this by all means is the only way to get the sum needed for his sister’s surgery. However, incidentally not only does he lose this chance, but he also becomes a reckless criminal, whose goal is to stop the process of turning people into ruck.
