About The Book

The fifth edition of the Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders book was published by the American Psychiatric Association in 2013. This book was written based on the health and pharmaceuticals companies, psychiatric agencies and other establishments' researches. The first publication was revealed in 1952. Since that time, the book received many additional materials, it was revised by specialists, who removed or updated the necessary information about all types and diagnosis of mental disorders. This manual is a hard work of many authors and is the most complete, up-to-date resource for clinical experience, which are able to classify and diagnose mental disorders. This book is valuable for many doctors, psychologists, physicians, therapists and other professional people who work in the sphere of health service.
